Privacy Policy

At imagetoolify, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our image and PDF tools.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. All images and PDFs are processed directly in your browser and are never uploaded to our servers.

  • No image or PDF files are stored.
  • No user registration or login is required.
  • Personal details like name or email are collected only when you contact us.

Cookies & Advertising

We may use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes such as Google AdSense. Cookies help us improve user experience and show relevant ads.

Third-Party Services

Third-party services like Google Analytics or AdSense may collect non-personal data according to their own privacy policies.

Children’s Information

imagetoolify does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.
